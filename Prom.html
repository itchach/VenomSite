In the cold silence of space, an alien organism drifts
toward Earth… a living shadow searching for a host.

It has no name, no purpose—only hunger.

Brock stands alone in the shadows, tense and alert, unaware of the lurking danger approaching.

The black symbiote strikes, wrapping around him.
Brock struggles, fighting to break free,
but the alien force begins to consume him.

The symbiote spreads, merging with his body.
Brock’s form is partially lost as the living shadow begins to take shape.

Venom’s humanoid shape emerges, tendrils writhing and flowing outward.
Brock is nearly swallowed, his struggle fading.

Symbiote Lore

From the dark reaches of the cosmos, symbiotes are living alien organisms capable of bonding with a host, amplifying strengths and weaknesses alike. Some crave chaos, others seek protection—but all leave a permanent mark on humanity.

Venom

The original symbiote. Adaptable and protective, balancing human morality with raw power.

Carnage

Chaotic and murderous, thrives on bloodlust, almost impossible to control.

Toxin

A blend of Venom’s protection and Carnage’s aggression, powerful but unpredictable.

Sleeper

Patient and stealthy, prefers ambush and strategy over brute force.

Anti-Venom

Purifying and redemptive, can neutralize other symbiotes and toxins.
